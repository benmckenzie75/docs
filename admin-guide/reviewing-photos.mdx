---
title: "Reviewing and Approving Photos"
description: "How to review, approve, and reject photo submissions in a session."
---

After capturers submit photos, they arrive in the **Submissions** tab of your session for review. As an admin or coordinator, you decide which photos are accepted.

## Viewing submissions

1. Open your session from the sidebar
2. Go to the **Submissions** tab
3. Use the status filter to focus on what needs attention:
   - **Pending** — New submissions awaiting your review
   - **Approved** — Photos you've already accepted
   - **Rejected** — Photos you've sent back for a retake

## Reviewing a photo

Click on any submission to see the full-size photo along with the person's details. From here you can:

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Approve** | Accept this photo as the current approved photo for the person |
| **Reject** | Send it back to the capturer with a reason (they receive an email notification) |
| **Request retake** | Ask the capturer to take a new photo, with specific notes about what to improve |

<Info>
Only **one photo per person** can be approved at a time. If you approve a new photo, the previous approved photo is automatically **superseded** — it's kept in the history but is no longer the active photo.
</Info>

## Rejection reasons

When you reject a photo, include a clear reason so the capturer knows what to fix. Common reasons:

- Poor lighting or harsh shadows
- Photo is blurry or out of focus
- Wrong person photographed
- Eyes closed
- Background too busy
- Subject not centred in frame

The capturer receives an email notification with your rejection reason and can submit a new photo.

## Bulk review tips

- Use the **Pending** filter to work through new submissions efficiently
- Photos are shown with the person's name and UID for easy identification
- The submission count on the Overview tab shows how many are pending review

## Photo statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Submitted, waiting for your review |
| **Approved** | Accepted as the current photo for this person |
| **Rejected** | Sent back to the capturer with a reason |
| **Retake Requested** | You've asked the capturer for a new photo |
| **Superseded** | Was previously approved, but a newer photo replaced it |

## What happens after approval

Approved photos are:
- Shown as the person's current photo throughout the session
- Available for **export** (ZIP download with custom filenames)
- Available for **ID card printing** via card templates
- Included when you **generate a ChangeSet** to sync back to your Master List
