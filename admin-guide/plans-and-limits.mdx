## Understanding limits

### People records

This is the total number of people across all your **active sessions and Master Lists**. Archived sessions don't count towards your limit.

<Info>
If you reach your people limit, you can still manage existing records — you just can't add new ones until you archive old sessions or upgrade your plan.
</Info>

### Storage

Storage is used by uploaded photos (originals and thumbnails). Typical usage:
- Each photo: approximately 500 KB – 1.5 MB
- 1,000 photos: approximately 1 – 2 GB

### Master Lists and Templates

These limits apply to **active** (non-archived) items. You can archive items you're no longer using to free up capacity.

## Managing your subscription

1. Go to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. View your current plan and usage
3. Click **Upgrade** or **Downgrade** to change plans
4. Manage payment details via the **Stripe Customer Portal**

### Upgrading

Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing period.

### Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Make sure your usage is within the new plan's limits before downgrading.

<Warning>
If your usage exceeds the new plan's limits when the downgrade takes effect, you'll enter a restricted state. You can still access and export your data, but you won't be able to create new sessions or add people until you're within limits.
</Warning>

### Cancellation

If you cancel your subscription:
- Your account enters a **read-only** state
- You can still sign in, view data, and export
- You cannot create new sessions, capture photos, or add people
- Your data is preserved — contact us if you want to reactivate

## Trial

Every new organisation gets a **14-day free Pro trial** — you get full access to Pro features including 3 Master Lists, 2,500 people, 10 Card Templates, and directory sync. No credit card required.

After the trial:
- **Subscribe to any plan** to continue using CaptrID
- If you don't subscribe within 3 days of trial expiry, your account enters read-only mode
- Features reduce to Starter limits if you don't subscribe, but **your data is never deleted**
- You can upgrade at any time to restore full access
